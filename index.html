A Rough Trade Writing School

Writing school for writers who mean it.

Not a productivity course. Not a content system. A writing school — built around Wild Magic, a set of techniques centered on trust, wonder, and learning to say the thing only you can say.

For writers of literary fiction, creative nonfiction, and the strange hybrid work that doesn't fit neatly in either category. For writers who are serious about the practice even when — especially when — the practice isn't going well.
